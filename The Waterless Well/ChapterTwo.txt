Liyue watched, no birds ever visited the well, no animals went near it, it only stood there, without company, day in day out.
It was then that an impulsive thought decided to grow into his curious mind. 

'I want to go down there.', and with a determined gaze, he took the first step, on to the steep slopes.

Liyue was the best traveller the town had to see. His curious mind was both a blessing and a curse, some would say. But, none of them would ever expect Liyue to make the dangerous trip down the mountains, just to satiate his curiosity.
